Ingredients:
 - 12 cup water
 - maggi 8 pack satchel

Directions:
  1. Boil water on medium flame in a pan.
  2. Introduce maggi cakes one by one after water is warm.
  3. Stir the mixture continuously until the cakes segregate into noodles.
  4. Heat until the required hardness of noodles is achieved.
  5. Add the spicy mixture to the pan and stir well until most of the liquid
     vaporizes.
